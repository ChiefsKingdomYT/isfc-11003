The Curiosity Mission

Curiosity is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Gale crater on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission.

Curiosity: View 1

Curiosity was launched from Cape Canaveral in 2011 and landed on Mars in 2012.

Curiosity: View 2

The rover's goals include an investigation of the Martian climate and geology.
